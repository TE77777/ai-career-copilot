# AI Career Copilot

AI Career Copilot is an experimental AI tool that analyzes how well a candidate's resume matches a job description.

The goal is to simulate how recruiters evaluate candidates and provide strategic insights to improve job applications.

---

# The Problem

Many professionals apply for jobs without understanding their real alignment with the role.

This leads to:

- low interview response rates
- weak resume positioning
- inefficient job search strategies

---

# The Solution

AI Career Copilot analyzes:

• Resume content  
• Job description requirements  

And generates:

- Candidate-job fit score
- Recruiter-style evaluation
- Strategic resume improvements
- Interview preparation insights

---

# Example Analysis

Score: **7.5 / 10**

Recruiter Insight:

"The candidate shows strong experience in SaaS products and cross-functional collaboration. However, the resume does not clearly highlight experience with CRM integrations and revenue-focused product metrics."

---

# Product Hypothesis

If candidates receive structured feedback on how their resume aligns with job descriptions, they can significantly improve their chances of getting interviews.
